About The Author:

Chirnus M Isidore has a passion for Evangelism, Marketing, Communication, and teaching new, innovative methods of sharing the good news of salvation. He hails from the Island of Saint Lucia and currently serves as a District Pastor with the Grenada Conference of Seventh-day Adventists on the picturesque Island of Grenada. Isidore is the holder of BA degree in the field of Religion from Northern Caribbean University, Jamaica. A Masters degree in Theological Studies from Liberty University Online, USA, and a Masters of Business Administration (MBA) in the areas of Finance and Accounting as well as Marketing from the University of the Southern, Trinidad.
